The Proposal

August 12th 2016

Lands End Cabo San Lucas Mexico

James proposed at Landâ€™s end in Cabo San Lucas on Friday August 12th, 2016. We had an amazing view of the beach from our suite. We had been in Cabo since Wednesday but Friday was the first time we actually set foot on the sand along the resort. We walked along the beach at sunset and once we got to Lands End I started to draw in the sand. We were all alone on the beach when James called my name to grab my attention. He asked for both of my hands, got down on one knee and asked me to marry him! I said yes and here we are. Ready to celebrate our big day with our closest friends and family.
